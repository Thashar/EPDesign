SERVICES

Comprehensive engineering services
for the energy sector

Specialist piping engineering services — from analyses and designs, through 3D scanning and modelling, to expert automation support and compliance verification with IRiESP and NC-RfG.

EPD-SRV-01

Pre-project analyses

Comprehensive preliminary studies and feasibility assessments prior to piping project execution. Route, layout and material optimisation for energy and industrial environments.

EPD-SRV-02

Piping flexibility analysis

FEM-based stress and flexibility analyses. Assessment of thermal, mechanical and dynamic stresses in accordance with EN, ASME and sector-specific energy standards.

EPD-SRV-03

Executive & as-built designs

Complete executive and as-built technical documentation compliant with industry standards. Isometrics, P&ID diagrams, material lists — for the energy and industrial sectors.

EPD-SRV-04

3D laser scanning

Precision laser scanning of existing industrial and energy installations. Generation of high-resolution point clouds as the basis for further design work.

EPD-SRV-05

3D installation modelling

Creation of accurate digital models of piping installations based on scans and documentation. PDMS, SP3D, E3D formats. Complete as-built documentation for asset management.

EPD-SRV-06

Automation — IRiESP & NC-RfG

Expert automation support at all project stages. Analysis and verification of control logics in compliance with IRiESP requirements and the European NC-RfG standard.

HISTORY

Piping engineering
driven by precision

Energy Piping Design Sp. z o.o. was established in 2017 in response to growing market demand for specialised piping engineering services in the energy sector. The company was founded by experienced engineers with extensive practice on the largest Polish and European energy projects.

In 2019, we expanded our offering to include advanced 3D laser scanning services, becoming one of the few companies in Poland combining design expertise with digital reproduction capabilities for existing installations.

In 2021, we established strategic partnerships with Western European companies, enabling the execution of cross-border projects and implementation of NC-RfG compliant methodologies on European markets. Today, EPDesign comprises more than 20 specialist engineers completing over a dozen projects annually for the energy, petrochemical and industrial sectors.
